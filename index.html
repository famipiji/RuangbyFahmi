About Me

We can make it together

I’m a freelance photographer who loves capturing real moments—whether it’s portraits, events, or everyday lifestyle shots. I enjoy playing with natural light, finding the right angles, and editing photos to bring out their best look.

I like working with people, making them feel comfortable in front of the camera, and turning simple moments into lasting memories. Photography for me isn’t just work, it’s a way to tell stories and share experiences through images.
